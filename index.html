About Me

"You’re just an imitation of what is around you, it’s just that you don’t imitate one person; you take bits and pieces of hundred people and make yourself.” -Sadhguru When I'm not working I'm playing tennis, finding the next mountaineering or kitesurfing trip, or as of lately being a cheerleader for my kids at their many tennis tournaments. I grew up in Atlanta and have been living in and around the city my whole life. I'm an engineer from Georgia Tech, who somehow found his way into financial and tax consulting. I've always been an early adopter for the right technology in each of my roles, and was most recently on my firm's advisor board to migrate all services to the cloud and develop business intelligence tools and automated bots to assist in analysis, data collection, and the provision of services.

I'm a dog lover/whisperer. I'm obsessed with the Gremlins movies from the 1980's and an an avid reader of space operas, non-fiction, thrillers, and the occasional history book on people like Marcus Aurelius or Ghengis Khan.
